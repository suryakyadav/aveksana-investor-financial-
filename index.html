Our Bottom-Up Plan

Our financial plan is a bottom-up forecast, built directly from our specific hiring plan and go-to-market strategy across our three revenue streams. Here is the story it tells:

1. The Plan (Money Out / Expenses)

  • New Funding Strategy: We start with €200k (consisting of a €100k bridge round that unlocks a €100k public grant).
  • Seed Round: The main €800,000 Seed round is scheduled to close in Q1 2026.
  • Base Cost: Our "lights-on" cost for 3 founders & cofounders + €12k in operations is roughly €30,000/month (including 25% payroll tax).
  • Full Burn Rate: Once our 5-person team is hired in H1 2026, our total monthly burn rate (Base Cost + New Hires) averages out to ~€55,000 per month.

2. The Result (Money In / Revenue)

Our growth is driven by three distinct revenue streams:

  • 1. B2C Thesis Support (The Base): We are already live and generating ~€300 MRR. This stream grows linearly as we scale from our current base to add 3,000–6,000 new users/month.
  • 2. B2B Universities (The Scale): Selling our Thesis Support System directly to institutions. We assume a ~4.5 month sales cycle with an average contract value (ACV) of €30k.
  • 3. B2B Artha (The Upsell): A specialized grant-writing module. Professors requested and paid us to build and pilot this tool. It now acts as a high-value upsell to our university contracts, with pricing tiers from €2.5k to €20k.

3. The Grand Finale (The KPIs)

The model aggregates these streams. It shows expenses ramping up first, followed by the compounding effect of our B2B sales engines (Universities + Artha) kicking in around Month 5.

  • Runway: Even accounting for the ~€90k burn during the Q4 2025 "Bridge Period," we have sufficient capital to reach our Series A milestones.
  • Year 1 Goal: By the end of the first 12 months, we successfully hit our target of ~€330,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR).
  • Year 2: The model continues this logic, showing growth to ~€900,000 ARR by the end of Month 24 as our team is fully ramped.

Our Investment Thesis

Our Value Proposition: We leverage a high-engagement B2C product to create a highly qualified, low-cost PQL funnel for our high-value B2B enterprise platforms (Thesis Support & Artha).

  • Phase 1 Goal Achieved: The model shows we will comfortably exceed our €25k MRR target within 12 months.
