Our Bottom-Up Plan

Our financial plan is a bottom-up forecast, built directly from our specific hiring plan and go-to-market strategy—not high-level guesses. Here is the story it tells:

1. The Plan (Money Out / Expenses)

  • Starting Point: We begin with €800,000 from the seed round.
  • Base Cost: Our "lights-on" cost for 3 founders & cofounders + €12k in operations is roughly €30,000/month (including 25% payroll tax).
  • The "Use of Funds": Our plan is to hire 5 new key people (3 Tech, 2 Commercial) in Q1 and Q2 of 2026.
  • Full Burn Rate: Once this 5-person team is hired, our total monthly burn rate (Base Cost + New Hires) averages out to ~€55,000 per month.

2. The Result (Money In / Revenue)

This new, larger team is what drives our revenue growth. The model projects two streams:

  • B2C Revenue (The Base): This stream grows linearly, driven by our marketing plan to add 3,000 to 6,000 new users per month. A small percentage (0.25%) of these convert, building a steady, predictable MRR base.
  • B2B Revenue (The Growth Driver): This is our main focus. Our new sales team has a 4.5-month ramp-up. Starting around Month 5, they begin to close 2-3 new €30,000/year deals per quarter. This is what creates the big jumps in revenue.

3. The Grand Finale (The KPIs)

The model puts these two pieces together. It shows our expenses ramp up first (as we hire the team) and then our B2B revenue follows as that new team starts to execute.

  • Runway: The €800,000 is designed to fund this 5-person team and plan for ~14.5 months.
  • Year 1 Goal: By the end of the first 12 months, we successfully hit our target of ~€330,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR).
  • Year 2: The model continues this logic, showing growth to ~€900,000 ARR by the end of Month 24 as our team is fully ramped.

Our Investment Thesis

Our Value Proposition: We leverage a high-engagement B2C product to create a highly qualified, low-cost PQL funnel for our high-value B2B enterprise platform.

  • Phase 1 Goal Achieved: The model shows we will comfortably exceed our €25k MRR target within 12 months.
