Our Bottom-Up Plan

Our financial plan is a bottom-up forecast, built directly from our specific hiring plan and go-to-market strategy across our three revenue streams.

1. The Plan (Money Out / Expenses)

  • New Funding Strategy: We start with €200k (consisting of a €100k bridge round that unlocks a €100k public grant).
  • Seed Round: The main Seed round is €1.5M (closing Q2 2026) to fuel aggressive engineering and sales growth.
  • Current Team (The Base): We are currently a team of 5, including 3 founders (covering AI, Product/Marketing, Sales) and 2 engineers (Fullstack & Junior). Our "lights-on" burn now is €9,000/mo.
  • Lean Start (Q1): We maintain this lean "lights-on" burn (~€9k/mo) through Q1 to maximize the bridge runway.
  • Phased Scaling: We will hire 7 new key team members spread across Q2 and Q3 to ramp up responsibly. Once fully hired, our total burn rate stabilizes at ~€70,000 per month.

2. The Result (Money In / Revenue)

Our growth is driven by three distinct revenue streams:

  • 1. B2C Thesis Support (The Base): We are already live and generating ~€300 MRR. This stream grows linearly as we scale from our current base to add 3,000–6,000 new users/month.
  • 2. B2B Universities (The Scale): Selling our Thesis Support System directly to institutions. We assume a ~4.5 month sales cycle with an average contract value (ACV) of €30k. closing 10 deals in Year 1 drives the majority of our €330k ARR target.
  • 3. B2B Artha (The Upsell): A specialized grant-writing module. Professors requested and paid us to build and pilot this tool. It now acts as a high-value upsell to our university contracts, with pricing tiers from €2.5k to €20k.

3. The Grand Finale (The KPIs)

Here is how the numbers come together. You’ll see an initial ramp in expenses as we assemble the team. This is deliberate. It builds the capacity that allows our B2B engines (Universities + Artha) to kick in around Month 5 and drive compounding growth.

  • Runway: With the robust €1.7M total funding (Bridge + Seed) and a phased hiring plan, we have secured a runway of ~24 months.
  • Year 1 Goal: By the end of the first 12 months, we successfully hit our target of ~€330,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR).
  • Year 2: The model continues this logic, showing growth to ~€900,000 ARR by the end of Month 24 as our team is fully ramped.

Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Risk Area Mitigation Strategy
Long Sales Cycles (Universities) Revenue Diversification: Our steady B2C revenue stream provides cash flow continuity, while Artha (departmental budget) offers a faster B2B sales cycle than full university-wide procurements.
AI Commoditization Data Moat: We aren't just an interface. We own the proprietary workflow data from students -> researchers, training our models to be increasingly specific and defensible against generic LLMs.
Hiring Delays Operational Buffer: Our financial plan assumes a phased, conservative hiring ramp. We have a 6-month buffer in our runway to absorb recruitment timelines without impacting survival.
